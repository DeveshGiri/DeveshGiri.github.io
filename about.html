About Me

I am Devesh Giri, pursuing a BS-MS degree at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune, India. I am currently a Visiting Master's Thesis Student at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) Mumbai, working on gravitational wave physics in the research group of Prof. Suvodip Mukherjee. I am interested in a spectrum of things, including Physics, Philosophy and Literature.

I take formal training in Mathematics and Physics at IISER-Pune. I am an ardent follower of Hindustani Sangeet. Besides science and music, I spend a lot of time reading in and around the domains of politics, philosophy, and logic. I write and love Hindi-Urdu poetry.

In the formal domain, I work on problems broadly related to Gravitational Wave Astrophysics and Cosmology. Besides science research, I am highly passionate about promoting awareness about science and analyzing science pedagogy.
